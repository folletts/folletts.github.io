Women's Club Soccer

This year we will employ a wide 4-3-3 formation as illustrated to the right. Using this formation, we will establish 3 clear 'lines' on the field; a defensive, midfield, and attacking line. In order for this to be effective, the following must be true...

  • The 2 fullbacks (LB & RB) must support the wingers (LW & RW) in front of them without neglecting defensive responsibilities, and must provide passing options in the wide areas for the centrally positioned players.

  • The defensive midfielder (CDM) must protect the central defenders (CD), fill gaps left by the fullbacks, and help maintain overall team shape.

  • The 2 central midfielders (CM) and the central defensive midfielder (CDM) must work toegether to control possession, support the front 3 attackers, and support the defenders behind them.

  • The 2 wingers and the centre forward/striker (CF) must find space around and behind the opposition defenders, requiring near-constant off-the-ball movement.

  • The 2 wingers must provide width and depth, so that the centrally located players have the space to receive the ball and influence the build up and attack.
